There are numerous place online to get NDoc support.

The best place to start is often the NDoc Wiki or by looking through the support archive at the NDoc SourceForge foundry where you will often turn up the answer you are looking for.

The NDoc Users's Mailing List

You can also search the the archives or send a message to the ndoc-users mailing list.

The NDoc Tracker Database

If you still can not find the answer you are looking for, NDoc maintains a Tracker database at SourceForge.net.

  • To submit a support request, visit the NDoc support page at SourceForge.net.
  • To submit a bug report, visit the NDoc bug tracker page at SourceForge.net.
  • If there is a feature you'd like to suggest, let us know at the Request For Enhancement page at SourceForge.net.
